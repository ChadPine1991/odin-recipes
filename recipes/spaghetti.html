Spaghetti and Meat Sauce

Description

This is the recipe for some good Spaghetti and Meat Sauce. Its great for family dinner and other special occasions. Pretty easy to make with some simple ingredients.

Once you've tried this recipe, you will never make spaghetti any other way. It takes a little time to simmer and cook, but the wait will be well worth it.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • salt
  • pepper
  • 1 can tomato sauce and paste
  • 1 can beef broth
  • sugar
  • basil
  • oregano
  • garlic
  • Box of spaghetti noodles
  • parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Put salt, pepper, tomato sauce and paste, beef broth, sugar, basil, oregano, and garlic into a pot
  2. Using the broth can, add about 1 can of water into the pot
  3. Mix it all up and let it simmer on low heat for about 45 minutes to an hour
  4. Put a seperate pan on medium head and cook up the ground beef
  5. Once cooked, put the ground beef into the sauce and let it simmer
  6. 20 mintues before the sauce is done, cook box of noodles as directed on the package
  7. Drain Noodles
  8. Put noodles out and add the sauce on top
  9. Optional: Add Noodles to the sauce
  10. Add parmesan cheese on top and enjoy
