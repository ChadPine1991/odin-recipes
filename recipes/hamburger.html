Hamburger

Description

This is the recipe for a Hamburger. The Cheese will be optional. Going to run thru the basics of how you can make a great hamburger. Its great for any event whether its a huge party for the game or just a family night.

Ingredients

  • Buns
  • 1 lb Hamburger Meat
  • lettuce
  • tomatoes
  • onions
  • pickles
  • salt/pepper
  • season salt
  • mayo ketchup mustard
  • Thousand Island Dressing
  • cheese (options may vary)

Steps

  1. Bring a pan to medium heat
  2. Prep hamburger meat in a bowl with season salt, salt, and pepper
  3. Mix everything together
  4. Make into a ball shape
  5. Smash patties onto pan
  6. Cook patties to your liking
  7. As patties cook cut up the condiments
  8. Check on Patty, to your preference flip patty
  9. Optional: Place preferred cheese slice on top of patty
  10. Toast hamburger buns
  11. Once toasted put mayo ketchup mustard and Thousand Island onto bun
  12. Turn stove and place burger onto bun
  13. Place condiments on top of burger
  14. Enjoy burger
